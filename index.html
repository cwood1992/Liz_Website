About Liz R.

Elizabeth (“Liz”) R. is a passionate dance instructor and founder of Liz R. Dance Institute. With years of experience teaching students of all backgrounds, Liz is dedicated to creating a supportive, energetic, and fun learning environment for everyone—from first-time dancers to advanced performers.
